Help & Tips

Getting Started with BudgetMaster

Follow these simple steps to create your first budget:

Step 1: Enter Your Income

Start by entering your total monthly income. This is your baseline for all budget calculations.

Step 2: Add Fixed Expenses

List all your fixed monthly expenses like rent, insurance, loan payments, etc.

Step 3: Budget by Category

Allocate money to different spending categories. You can modify existing categories or add custom ones to match your lifestyle.

Step 4: Track Your Remaining Budget

The app automatically calculates how much money you have left after all expenses. Aim to have a positive remaining amount each month.

Money Management Best Practices

  • Review your budget monthly and adjust categories as needed
  • Try to save 10-20% of your income for emergency funds
  • Track actual spending against your budget categories
  • Build an emergency fund equivalent to 3-6 months of expenses
  • Download and keep records of your budgets for reference
